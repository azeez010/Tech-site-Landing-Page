About Us

Smart Routing System is a security-focused software company geared towards introducing an innovative security solution to the education space. Our solutions are based on respect for students and learning area protection, and a respect for privacy. Our innovations are practical, easy and quick to employ for our clients needing adequate protection. Our organisation is boosted by the knowledge of technology professionals with vast experience in managing and implementing security system initiatives.

We follow guiding principles and strive to deliver technology solutions that are effective in meeting security needs.
